About Pulsar150

Pulsar 150 is powered by 149.5cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 13.8 bhp and a torque of 13.25 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Bajaj Pulsar 150 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Pulsar 150 bike weighs 148 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 15 liters.
